HIP HOP 🔥

Urban Street Dance Culture

What is Hip Hop?

Hip Hop dance is a powerful urban dance style born in the Bronx, New York, during the 1970s. It's all about self-expression, rhythm, and energy.

🎵 Music

Strong beats, rap, and breakbeats. The music drives every movement.

💪 Energy

High intensity, sharp movements, and freestyle creativity.

🌍 Origin

Created by African American and Latino youth in New York City streets.

Popular Hip Hop Styles

  • Breaking: Floor moves, spins, and power moves
  • Popping: Muscle contractions to create jerky movements
  • Locking: Freezing in positions and sudden movements
  • Freestyle: Improvisation and personal style
